Tick is a simple, focused time-tracking app designed to help you see where your time goes.

Create a free account or login to start using.

Key features

  • One-click time tracking: start a timer on any task to begin a session, stop it when you’re done.
  • Automatic session logging: no manual entry required (but you can adjust durations afterward).
  • At-a-glance progress: always know how much time you’ve already invested in each task and project.
  • Project archiving: hide old projects from active lists without losing their history.
  • Daily summary: see what you worked on, how long you spent, and in what order.
  • Weekly and monthly summaries: view charts of daily focus time and project breakdowns.

Why use tick

Tick helps you stay aware of your focused work time: project by project, task by task. Start a timer, work without distractions, and review your progress with simple daily, weekly, and monthly summaries.

Whether you’re managing personal projects or keeping track of client work, Tick makes it simple to record your time, review your habits, and keep a complete history without cluttering your active workspace.
